Our Story

Welcome to S&S Footwear, a passion-driven business proudly rooted in Sri Lanka.
Founded by M.S. Kumararathna and Suneetha Rajapaksha, the name S&S reflects their shared initials and united vision.
What began as a heartfelt mission to create high-quality, comfortable, and stylish footwear has grown into
a trusted local brand, shaped by teamwork and dedication. Our journey started with humble beginnings, transforming a private home into our first workshop with just a small team. Despite early setbacks like staff turnover and material shortages, our co-founders pushed forward, personally overseeing operations, training staff, and building strong ties with local suppliers. These efforts laid the foundation for our independent manufacturing and commitment to consistent quality. Today, S&S Footwear continues to grow in popularity thanks to our focus on craftsmanship and customer satisfaction. We proudly share our latest designs through platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp Business, making it easy for you to connect with us. As our founders believe, a happy customer is our best advertisement, and your satisfaction is the true measure of our success.
